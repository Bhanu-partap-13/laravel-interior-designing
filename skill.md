# Yadav Interior — Designer Portfolio & Discovery Platform

## 🌟 Overview
**Yadav Interior** is a premium, curated digital platform designed to bridge the gap between world-class interior designers and clients seeking inspired spaces. It serves as a narrative-driven portfolio system where designers can showcase their craft through detailed case studies, and clients can discover verified talent based on style, budget, and location.

## 🚀 The Problem It Solves
1. **The Trust Gap**: Clients often struggle to verify the quality and experience of interior designers. Yadav Interior uses verified profiles and detailed project histories to build confidence.
2. **Budget Uncertainty**: Traditional portfolios often hide costs. We prioritize clear budget ranges and timelines on every project to help clients make informed decisions.
3. **Fragmented Discovery**: Instead of searching through social media, clients have a centralized hub filtered by specialized design fields (Living Room, Kitchen, Luxury, etc.).
4. **Designer Workflow**: Many designers lack a professional space to manage client inquiries. Our integrated dashboard simplifies the path from project discovery to direct inquiry.

## ✨ Current Feature Set
### For Designers
- **Premium Dashboard**: A centralized hub to track project performance and profile completeness.
- **Dynamic Portfolio**: Create and manage detailed project case studies with high-quality media, before/after comparisons, and style tags.
- **Inline Profile Management**: Update professional details (Bio, Specialization, Education, etc.) directly on the dashboard without page reloads.
- **Smart Photo Uploads**: Real-time photo preview and hover-overlays for easy profile picture management.
- **Safe Deletion**: A "Type to Confirm" deletion system to prevent accidental loss of portfolio work.

### For Clients
- **Curated Discovery**: Browse the latest work from verified studios on the homepage.
- **Category-Led Navigation**: Instant access to specialized design fields through a rich, icon-based category grid.
- **Project Transparency**: View detailed project breakdowns including duration, budget, and materials.
- **Direct Inquiry**: Connect with designers instantly through integrated contact forms on both project and profile pages.

### Core Experience
- **Multi-Language Support**: Fully translated into English, Hindi, and Tamil.
- **Dark Mode Excellence**: A sophisticated UI that respects system preferences and enhances visual storytelling.
- **High Performance**: Optimized image handling and local SVG placeholders ensure a fast, reliable experience.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Backend**: Laravel 11 (PHP 8.2+)
- **Frontend**: Blade Templating, Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Styling**: Custom Modern CSS (CSS Variables, Flexbox/Grid)
- **Database**: MySQL / MariaDB
- **Assets**: Vite for efficient asset bundling

## 🔮 Future Roadmap
- **Interactive Budget Calculator**: Help clients estimate project costs based on room size and material preferences.
- **3D/360° Gallery Support**: Allow designers to upload immersive walkthroughs of their finished spaces.
- **Advanced Search Algorithm**: A "Matchmaker" system that pairs clients with designers based on budget and stylistic compatibility.
- **Client Favorites/Wishlist**: Enable clients to "Save" specific projects or designers to a personal mood board.
- **Verified Review System**: A post-project review cycle to maintain the highest quality standards on the platform.
